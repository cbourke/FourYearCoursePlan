    CSE Four Year Course Planner

    Let's get started...

    To help you design a basic academic course plan, we need to ask a few questions first. Don't worry, you can always change your schedule if you need to. This is just to help you get started so you don't have to do all the work yourself. However, if you'd rather, you can just start from scratch.

    Tell us a little about your academic background and we can help build an initial schedule just for you.

    Which degree are you seeking?
    Which Semester will you begin your studies?
    How many years of a foreign language did you take in high school?
    Which Math course did you place in in the Math Placement Exam?
